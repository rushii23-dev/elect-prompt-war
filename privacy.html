Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026 — This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

1. Introduction

Your Vote, Your Voice ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide: name, email address, and any information you submit when contacting us or downloading resources.
  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, and timestamps collected by server logs and analytics.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: small files stored on your device to remember preferences and enable site features.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the site and its features.
  • Improve the site and develop new features based on usage patterns.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Send administrative messages (e.g., policy updates).

4. Legal Basis and Sharing

Where required by law, we process data based on your consent, performance of a contract, or our legitimate interests (such as site security and improvement). We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, email delivery) under contractual obligations to protect your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential site functionality and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling certain cookies may reduce site functionality.

6. Data Retention and Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or to comply with legal obligations. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data depending on your jurisdiction. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under applicable age limits. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will delete it.

9. International Transfers

Information may be processed in countries other than your residence. We apply appropriate safeguards to protect personal information transferred across borders.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be posted with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us at: privacy@yourvoteyourvoice.example.
